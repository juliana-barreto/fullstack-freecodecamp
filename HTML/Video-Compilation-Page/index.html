Cybersecurity

Cybersecurity is no longer just a concern for businesses—individuals are equally vulnerable to data breaches, scams, and privacy invasions. With cyber threats evolving daily, understanding basic protection methods is crucial. Below are three key topics with handpicked YouTube videos (from trusted channels) to help you stay safe online.

How to Create & Manage Secure Passwords

Weak passwords are a hacker’s easiest entry point. This Computerphile video explains why most passwords fail and how hackers crack them. It shows why long passphrases (like "correcthorsebatterystaple") beat complex short passwords, and recommends password managers for better security. Essential viewing for safer logins!

VPNs: What They Are & When to Use Them

A VPN (Virtual Private Network) is a powerful tool to secure your connection, especially on public networks. ProtonVPN's video simply explains how VPNs encrypt your connection to protect against hackers and tracking, while showing when they're most useful (public Wi-Fi, censorship bypass). Great starter guide!

How to Avoid Online Scams

Phishing, fake emails, and cloned websites are common traps. This video teaches you how to spot and evade scams, revealing criminal tactics and practical tips to verify links and messages before clicking.
